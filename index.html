Dộjima

Dojima network is an advanced decentralized technology built by the people of DojimaLand to decentralize the Traditional Finanancial instruments by solving the existing inefficiencies and make their transition easy.

Decentralized Financial Instruments

Dojima chain is built to be at the forefront of creating new age financial instruments and to decentralize traditional financial instruments

Faster Transactions

Dojima chain aims to enable faster transaction and keep on innovating to make transaction execution faster.

Low Cost

Dojima chain aims to reduce the cost of transaction on network to enable transactions at scale.

Mitigate Congestion

Dojima chain aims to mitigate the congestion problems that exist in present systems at times of market turmoil.

In Built Oracle

Dojima Chain is the first to implements an oracle service in nodes , making it Decentralized and solving the age old Oracle problem in decentralized networks.

Multi Chain Compatibility

Dojima chain lets users to transact and communicate with any Blockchain starting with Major Blockchains.
